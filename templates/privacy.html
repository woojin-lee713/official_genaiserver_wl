How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Provide and Maintain Our Services: We use your data to deliver our chatbot services, troubleshoot issues, and enhance functionality. This includes using the information to authenticate you, manage your account, and provide customer support.
  • Personalize Your Experience: By understanding your preferences and interaction patterns, we can tailor our responses and services to better suit your needs.
  • Improve Our Services: Usage data helps us identify trends, monitor performance, and make necessary improvements to our services. We use this information to develop new features and enhance existing ones.
  • Communicate With You: We may use your contact information to send updates, respond to inquiries, and provide customer support. We may also send promotional materials, although you can opt-out of these communications at any time.
  • Ensure Security and Prevent Fraud: We use your information to detect, prevent, and address security issues and fraudulent activities. This helps us protect our users and maintain the integrity of our services.
  • Comply With Legal Obligations: In some cases, we may be required to use or disclose your information to comply with legal obligations, such as responding to legal processes or government requests.

Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and implement various measures to protect it:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols. This ensures that your data remains confidential and protected from unauthorized access during transmission.
  • Access Control: Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel who have signed confidentiality agreements. We ensure that our employees and contractors undergo regular training on data protection practices.
  • Regular Security Audits: We conduct regular security audits and vulnerability assessments to identify and address potential threats. Our security team continuously monitors our systems for any unusual activity and takes immediate action to mitigate risks.
  • Secure Infrastructure: Our servers are hosted in secure data centers that comply with industry standards for physical and digital security. These data centers are equipped with state-of-the-art security measures, including 24/7 surveillance, biometric access controls, and fire suppression systems.
  • Data Anonymization: Whenever possible, we anonymize personal data to protect your privacy further. Anonymized data cannot be linked back to you and is used to improve our services and conduct research.
  • Incident Response: In the event of a data breach, we have an incident response plan to contain and mitigate the impact. We will notify affected users promptly and take steps to prevent future incidents.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you. We will provide this information within a reasonable timeframe and in a commonly used electronic format.
  • Correction: You have the right to request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete information. We will promptly update any incorrect information to ensure it is accurate and current.
  • Deletion: You can ask us to delete your personal data under certain circumstances. This includes when the data is no longer necessary for the purposes it was collected, if you withdraw your consent, or if you object to the processing and there are no overriding legitimate grounds for processing.
  • Objection: You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data. If you raise an objection, we will stop processing your information unless we can demonstrate compelling legitimate grounds for the processing.
  • Restriction: You can request that we restrict the processing of your personal information in certain situations. This includes when you contest the accuracy of the data, the processing is unlawful, or we no longer need the data but you require it for legal claims.
  • Portability: You have the right to request the transfer of your personal data to another service provider in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw Consent: If we rely on your consent to process your personal information, you have the right to withdraw your consent at any time. This will not affect the lawfulness of processing based on consent before its withdrawal.

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@ellishgpt.com. We will respond to your request within a reasonable timeframe and provide the necessary assistance.
